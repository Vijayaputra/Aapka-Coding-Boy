MARQUEE ATTRIBUTES

  1. bgcolor-eg.bgcolor=red(colorname).
  2. height-eg,height=30px .
  3. width-eg.width=100%
  4. direction-eg.diraction=right (right,left,up,down).
  5. behavior-behavior=alternate (right to left & left to right)
    behavior=scroll (right to left)
    behavior=slide (right to left & stop)
